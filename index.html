Elephant Rock

Elephant rock is a beautiful natural park. It was the first handicap accesible trail in Missouri. The parths are paved allowing users of any physical level to enjoy there hike or walk.

The Mountains have an extremly great view. From the top you have an amazing site. You can look around from the or any of the paths getting a good view of the valley.

There arte many other mountains around. The rocks have an elephant shape to them that give the name of the location Elephant Rocks.
